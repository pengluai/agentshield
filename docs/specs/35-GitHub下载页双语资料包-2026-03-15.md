## 2. Product Positioning
### 中文定位
AgentShield 是一款专门保护本机 AI 工具生态的桌面安全应用。它不是传统杀毒软件，而是帮助零基础用户看清本机哪些 AI 工具、MCP、Skill 和本地配置正在运行，哪些存在密钥泄露、危险权限和高风险自动化问题。

### English Positioning
AgentShield is a desktop safety app for local AI tool ecosystems. It is not a traditional antivirus. It helps beginners see which AI tools, MCP servers, Skills, and local configs are running on the machine, and which of them may expose secrets, overreach permissions, or trigger unsafe automation.

## 3. What Problem It Solves
### 中文
很多人装了越来越多 AI 工具后，真正危险的并不是“模型本身”，而是本机里看不见的配置、插件、密钥文件和高危自动化：
- 不知道电脑里到底有哪些 AI 工具在接入 MCP / Skill
- 不知道哪个插件能删文件、读密钥、外发数据
- 配置和权限出问题时，不知道从哪里查
- 试了很多 AI 工具，最后电脑环境越来越乱，风险越来越隐蔽

AgentShield 把这些问题从“看不见、看不懂、来不及处理”，变成“先发现、先提醒、再决定要不要放行”。

### English
As more AI tools get installed, the hidden risk is often not the model itself, but the local configs, plugins, secret files, and high-risk automations around it:
- You do not know which AI tools on your machine are using MCP or Skill
- You do not know which add-on can delete files, read secrets, or send data out
- When configs or permissions go wrong, you do not know where to look
- After trying many AI tools, the local environment becomes messy and risk becomes harder to see

AgentShield turns that from invisible, confusing, and too-late-to-fix into discover first, review first, then decide what to allow.

## 6. Recommended Release Copy Structure
建议 GitHub Release 页面使用下面这套顺序：

1. 这是什么 App
2. 它解决什么问题
3. 它现在能做什么
4. 这版下载包含什么
5. 重要边界和当前阶段说明

原因：
- 下载用户先判断“这跟我有没有关系”
- 然后判断“它是不是能解决我现在的问题”
- 最后才会看技术功能和版本说明

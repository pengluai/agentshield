# AgentShield Refund Policy
Last Updated: 2026-03-13

This policy describes how refunds are handled for AgentShield activation-code purchases.

## 1. Scope
This applies to purchases made through the official checkout channels.

## 2. Refund Window
- Recommended default: within 14 calendar days from purchase.
- If local law requires a longer period, legal requirements take precedence.

## 3. Eligibility
Refund requests may be approved when:
- activation failed due to verified product-side issues;
- duplicate purchase was made in error;
- other statutory rights apply.

Refunds may be denied for abuse, fraud, or policy violations.

## 4. How to Request
Send request to `support@your-domain.com` with:
- purchase email
- order ID
- issue description
- optional screenshots/log snippets

## 5. Processing Time
Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days, depending on payment method and provider.

## 6. License Impact
When a refund is approved and webhook confirmation arrives, AgentShield may automatically revoke associated activation rights.

## 7. Chargebacks
If a chargeback is initiated, the associated license may be suspended while the dispute is in review.

## 8. Policy Changes
We may update this policy and will publish the latest version with an updated date.

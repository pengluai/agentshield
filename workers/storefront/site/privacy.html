Product: AgentShield

Privacy requests: pengluailll@gmail.com

2. Data We Process

  • Account and purchase data such as email, order ID, plan, and payment status from the checkout provider.
  • License data including activation metadata, issue / reissue / revoke status, and expiry time.
  • App diagnostics such as crash logs, operation logs, scan summary statistics, and webhook failures.
  • Optional support data you send while requesting help.

3. Data We Do Not Collect by Default

  • We do not upload your local files, MCP configs, or key values by default.
  • Key Vault secrets stay in the local system keychain unless you explicitly export or share them.

4. Purposes of Processing

  • Deliver license activation and reissue.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized activations.
  • Provide security updates, incident notifications, and support.
  • Meet legal obligations such as tax, accounting, and compliance duties.

5. Third Parties

  • Merchant of Record and checkout: Creem.
  • Transactional email delivery: Resend.
  • Hosting and logging providers used by the license gateway and release infrastructure.

6. Security Controls

  • Signed webhook verification.
  • Hashed activation-code storage.
  • Authenticated admin operations for manual reissue and revoke actions.
  • Restricted access to production secrets and signing keys.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction. Contact pengluailll@gmail.com with the subject AgentShield Privacy Request.

8. Changes

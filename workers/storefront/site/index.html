macOS Installation Guide

Step 1: Download the .dmg file, open it, and drag the app to Applications.

Step 2: If macOS says “can’t be opened,” “can’t be verified,” or “is damaged”:

① Double-click AgentShield once in Applications (so macOS records the block)

② Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → click "Open Anyway"

③ Enter your Mac password, then click "Open" again

④ If still blocked, also try: Right-click (or Control + click) → "Open"

* “Open Anyway” usually appears for about 1 hour after your first blocked launch attempt. After approving once, future launches are normal.
